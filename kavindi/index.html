Sunset at Sunset Cliffs

Ladera St & Cornish Dr, Ocean Beach

Sunset at 7:07 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps
golden hour • dramatic cliffs • the whole reason you came

Dramatic cliff formations, coastal arches, and unobstructed Pacific views. The trail is easy and mostly flat — no strenuous hiking, just a calm walk along the bluffs. Arrive around 5:45 to explore, find a spot to sit, and settle in for golden hour.

Bring: A blanket or towel to sit on. A light jacket for the ocean breeze.

Parking: Free dirt lots off Ladera St and Cornish Dr. Also free street parking along Sunset Cliffs Blvd. Arrive before 6 PM — spots fill up closer to sunset.

🔄 Smooth Swaps

If OB Noodle House is packed: Walk to The Joint OB at 4902 Newport Ave — great sushi and tapas, happy hour daily 12–6 PM. Same OB vibe, still within budget.

If An’s Gelato has a long line: Hit Lighthouse Ice Cream at 5059 Newport Ave — family-owned, gourmet scoops, open Mon 12–8 PM.

If weather turns cloudy: Skip the beach stroll, drive straight to Sunset Cliffs and cozy up for a moody sunset instead.

Coast Walk Trail & Seal Watching

La Jolla Cove & Children’s Pool

2:30 – 3:30 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps
scenic • easy • opens the date with awe

The Coast Walk Trail is a 0.6-mile cliffside path with nonstop ocean views, sea lion sounds, and kayakers below. Flat, easy, and the opposite of strenuous. After the trail, walk over to Children’s Pool to watch harbor seals sunbathing on the beach from the seawall above.

Free. No ticket, no time limit. Just show up and walk.

~10 min stroll through La Jolla Village to the restaurant

Late Lunch at King of Thai Cuisine

7734 Girard Ave, La Jolla

4:00 – 5:00 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps Call Menu
authentic Thai • bold flavors • Girard Ave charm

Authentic Thai right on La Jolla’s main street. The Pad Thai, Thai Mango Chicken, and curries are all boldly flavored — nothing bland here. Fresh ingredients, and the kind of local spot you wouldn’t find on your own.

Timing note: They close for a break 3–4 PM on Mondays, then reopen at 4 PM. That’s why you stroll the village after the Coast Walk and arrive right when they reopen.

Monday hours: 11 AM – 3 PM, 4 PM – 9 PM.

Sunset at Scripps Park

1100 Coast Blvd, La Jolla

Sunset at 7:07 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps
grassy bluffs • cove views • golden light

A 5.6-acre park on rocky bluffs right next to La Jolla Cove. Find a spot on the grass, sit together, and watch the sky light up. The green belvedere huts are great for photos. You have almost two hours here — no rush, no agenda. This is the kind of quality time that makes a date stick.

Free. Open space, ocean sounds, and nowhere you need to be.

🔄 Smooth Swaps

If King of Thai has a wait: Walk to Pho La Jolla at 3211 Holiday Ct — about a 10 min drive toward UTC. Great Vietnamese pho, open straight through, very affordable.

If Bobboi is closed or packed: Grab boba at Happy Lemon in Westfield UTC — about a 10 min drive. Or keep strolling Girard Ave and grab coffee instead.

If Coast Walk feels short: Add the Scripps Coastal Meander Trail near Birch Aquarium for more cliffside ocean views.
