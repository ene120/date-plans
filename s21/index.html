The Format

How the Show Works

Each round features one guy and one girl. This process repeats for all three pairings.

Round 1

His Spotlight

The guy comes on stage first, blindfolded, alone. For about 10 minutes, the host and cohost will ask him questions, most of them personal, so the audience can get to know who he really is.

Round 2

Her Spotlight

The guy puts on headphones so he can't hear anything. The girl comes on stage, also blindfolded, alone. She gets the same treatment: about 10 minutes of questions so the audience gets to know her too.

Round 3

The Blind Date

Both the guy and the girl are on stage together, both blindfolded. They go on a date right there in front of the audience. The host and cohost guide the conversation with questions. If any question makes you uncomfortable, give a short answer and the host will move on. No pressure.

Round 4

The Audience Wildcard

Before the blindfolds come off, audience members who are interested in either contestant can text the cohost. If selected, they'll walk up on stage and pitch themselves to you directly. This means even though you already have a match sitting next to you, someone in the crowd might shoot their shot.

Round 5

The Reveal

If the chemistry is right between the two contestants on stage, you'll be asked if you want to kiss or hug your date before the blindfolds come off, and again after. This only happens if the vibe is clearly there.

After The Show

The Mixer

Once all three rounds are done, there's a break period where everyone mingles. Whether your on-stage date went perfectly or not, this is another chance to connect with someone new.

Backstage

Arrival & Behind the Scenes

🍺 Your Green Room

When you arrive, girls and guys will be in separate rooms. Each room has its own bathroom, snacks, and non-alcoholic drinks. We have a bartender on site. You'll be asked what drink you want and it will be brought to you in your room. Someone will be checking in on both rooms throughout the evening to make sure you're comfortable and have everything you need.

🚶 Moving Around

You won't be stuck in your room the entire time. Girls and guys will be given alternating times to leave their rooms and walk around the house. However, you will never be allowed to go downstairs where the show is happening until it's your turn to go on stage. The guys and girls will not be out at the same time.

🪑 The Seating

You'll be sitting on high chairs on stage during your date. Just a heads up, especially for the girls. Plan your outfit accordingly. You'll want to be comfortable and confident up there, so keep the high chairs in mind when picking your look.

🥷 The Blindfold

Right before you reach the backyard/stage area, you'll put on your blindfold. You can look down at the ground through the bottom of the blindfold to navigate the stairs safely. Once you're near the stage, do your best not to look at the audience. The show is about you and your date. Forget the crowd. Be yourself. Go as crazy as you want.

Fair Warning

About Your Host

⚠️ Expect the Unexpected

Let's get one thing straight. Your host might say things that completely catch you off guard. Some of it will be wild. Some of it will be unhinged. Some of it will make you wonder if this was even approved by anyone. That's the point.

This show isn't just about the dates. It's a show. The audience came to laugh, to gasp, and to have the time of their lives. So if Qudsi goes off the rails, just know it's all to keep the energy through the roof and give everyone in that room an unforgettable night. Don't be shocked. Be entertained. And if you can, play along, because when you lean into the chaos, that's when the magic happens.

"If you're not a little bit nervous about what might come out of the host's mouth, you haven't been paying attention."
