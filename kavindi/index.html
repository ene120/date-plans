Sunset at Sunset Cliffs

Sunset at 7:07 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps

This is the reason we built the whole evening around this time of day. Every stop before this was timed so you’d arrive here right as the light turns golden. Dramatic cliff formations, coastal arches, and unobstructed Pacific views. The trail is easy and mostly flat so you can just take it all in. We recommend arriving around 5:45 to explore, find a spot to sit, and settle in together.

Bring: A blanket or towel to sit on. A jacket for the ocean breeze.

Parking: Free dirt lots off Ladera St and Cornish Dr. Also free street parking along Sunset Cliffs Blvd. Arrive before 6 PM. Spots fill up closer to sunset.

🔄 Just in Case

We always have a backup ready for you. If OB Noodle House is packed: Walk to The Joint OB at 4902 Newport Ave. Great sushi and tapas, happy hour daily 12 to 6 PM. Same OB vibe, still within budget.

If An’s Gelato has a long line: Head to Lighthouse Ice Cream at 5059 Newport Ave. Family-owned, gourmet scoops, open Mon 12 to 8 PM.

If weather turns cloudy: Skip the beach stroll, drive straight to Sunset Cliffs and enjoy a moody sunset together instead.

Coast Walk Trail & Seal Watching

2:30 to 3:30 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps

We open with this on purpose. Starting active builds natural energy between you two before you sit down to eat. This 0.6-mile cliffside path has nonstop ocean views, sea lion sounds, and kayakers below. It’s flat, easy, and the opposite of strenuous. After the trail, walk over to Children’s Pool to watch harbor seals sunbathing from the seawall above.

Free. No ticket, no time limit. Just show up and enjoy it together.

~10 min stroll through La Jolla Village to the restaurant

Sunset at Scripps Park

Sunset at 7:07 PM
Google Maps Apple Maps

Everything before this was building to this moment. A 5.6-acre park on rocky bluffs right next to La Jolla Cove. Find a spot on the grass with gelato, sit together, and watch the sky light up. The green belvedere huts are great for photos. You have almost two hours here with no rush and no agenda.

Free. Ocean sounds, open space, and nowhere you need to be.

🔄 Just in Case

We always have a backup ready for you. If King of Thai has a wait: Walk to Pho La Jolla at 3211 Holiday Ct. About a 10 min drive toward UTC. Great Vietnamese pho, open straight through, very affordable.

If Bobboi is closed or packed: Grab boba at Happy Lemon in Westfield UTC. About a 10 min drive. Or keep strolling Girard Ave and grab coffee instead.

If Coast Walk feels short: Add the Scripps Coastal Meander Trail near Birch Aquarium for more cliffside ocean views.
