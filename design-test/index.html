Plan 01

Golden Hour

Laid-back OB vibes, bold food, and a sunset you'll remember

Date
Mon, Mar 30
Duration
~5 hours
Area
Ocean Beach

The Vibe

Bold Vietnamese food at a local institution, the #1 ranked ice cream in the country, a walk along the OB Pier, and a golden-hour sunset on the cliffs. Thoughtful and well-timed without feeling overproduced — you end the night watching the sun drop into the Pacific together.

Mira Mesa → OB Noodle House → An's Gelato → OB Pier → Sunset Cliffs → Home

Weather — March 30

Mid-60s afternoon cooling to upper 50s by sunset. Ocean breezes pick up at the coast — bring a light jacket. Rain unlikely, but check forecast a few days before.

1

Late Lunch at OB Noodle House

2218 Cable St, Ocean Beach

(619) 450-6868

3:00 – 4:15 PM
Directions Call Menu

local legend · bold flavors · happy hour

A genuine OB institution featured on Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives. The pho, fried rice, and crispy egg noodles are all boldly flavored and shareable. Arrive at 3 PM when happy hour kicks in — half-off select beers, $4 premium wells until 6 PM. Walk-in friendly.

Good move: Split an appetizer, get two entrees, and try each other's. Pho and Saigon fried rice are standouts.

Monday hours: 12 PM – 11 PM.

~10 min walk to next stop
2

Dessert at An's Dry Cleaning

1861 Bacon St, Ocean Beach

(619) 310-5028

4:30 – 5:00 PM
Directions Call

quirky · award-winning · unforgettable

Voted the #1 Ice Cream Shop in America by USA Today. Every flavor has a techy name like Walkman, Bluetooth, and Motherboard. The kind of spot that makes a date feel like a discovery.

Monday hours: 12 PM – 10 PM.

~5 min walk to next stop
3

Walk the OB Pier

Niagara Ave & Abbott St, Ocean Beach

5:05 – 5:30 PM
Directions

breezy · scenic · easy transition

The longest concrete pier on the West Coast. Walk out, feel the ocean breeze, watch the surfers. An easy, no-pressure transition between dessert and the sunset spot.

Free. No time limit.

~3 min drive to Sunset Cliffs
4

Sunset at Sunset Cliffs

Ladera St & Cornish Dr, Ocean Beach

5:45 – 6:45 PM
Directions

golden hour · cliffside · unforgettable

The finale. Sunset Cliffs is one of the most iconic sunset spots in San Diego — raw, unpolished cliffs with the Pacific stretching out below. Arrive 30–40 minutes before sunset.

Sunset on March 30: ~7:10 PM. Arrive by 6:30 PM for the best light.

Plan 02

Coastal Charm

La Jolla's best — trails, seals, Thai food, gelato, and a cove sunset

Date
Mon, Mar 30
Duration
~5.5 hours
Area
La Jolla

The Vibe

A scenic coastal walk with real ocean views, sea lions lounging below, Thai food on La Jolla's main strip, the best gelato in San Diego, and a sunset at the cove. Calmer and more scenic — perfect if you want the date to feel like an escape.

Mira Mesa → Coast Walk Trail → Seal Rock → Amarin Thai → Bobboi Gelato → La Jolla Cove → Home

Weather — March 30

Mid-60s along the coast, slight breeze near the water. Comfortable for walking. Layers recommended for the evening.

1

Coast Walk Trail

Coast Walk Trail, La Jolla

2:30 – 3:15 PM
Directions

hidden gem · ocean views · easy walk

A short, scenic trail tucked behind the La Jolla caves that most tourists miss. Ocean views, sea cave overlooks, and enough variety to keep the conversation going.

Free. Open sunrise to sunset.

~8 min walk
2

Seal Watching at Seal Rock

Coast Blvd, La Jolla

3:20 – 3:45 PM
Directions

adorable · easy · great photos

Sea lions lounging on the rocks, waves crashing — low effort, high reward. Hang at the railing, take some photos, enjoy the view.

~5 min walk
3

Late Lunch at Amarin Thai

1264 Prospect St, La Jolla

(858) 454-0525

3:50 – 5:00 PM
Directions Call

cozy · flavorful · walk-in friendly

A well-loved Thai spot on Prospect Street. Pad thai, green curry, and mango sticky rice are all excellent. Monday late lunch means no wait.

Monday hours: 11 AM – 9 PM.

~3 min walk
4

Gelato at Bobboi

8008 Girard Ave, La Jolla

5:05 – 5:30 PM
Directions

authentic Italian · best in SD

Widely considered the best gelato in San Diego. Made with Italian techniques and real ingredients. Get a cup and walk toward the cove.

~7 min walk
5

Sunset at La Jolla Cove

1100 Coast Blvd, La Jolla

5:45 – 7:00 PM
Directions

iconic · golden hour · perfect ending

La Jolla Cove at sunset — one of the most beautiful spots in San Diego. Find a bench or spot on the grass overlooking the water.

Sunset on March 30: ~7:10 PM. Arrive by 6:30 PM for golden hour.
