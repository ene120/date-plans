🎮 Plan 1

Playful & Dressed-Up

Polished without feeling stiff — intentional, fun, and easy

Date
Sunday, March 8, 2026
Duration
~5 hours
Area
North Park

🌹 The Vibe

This one feels polished without feeling stiff. You start with a cozy browse, slide into a more special dinner, get one genuinely fun activity after, then end with gelato before the short walk back to the car. It fits you both well because it feels intentional, gives you something to do after dinner, and still stays easy.

🚶 Route: Parking → Bookstore → Dinner → Arcade → Gelato → Car

Warm afternoon in the low 80s, settling into upper 60s at night. A light layer for later is enough — nice day for a dressed-up date. The walking parts will feel great and the evening stays comfortable.
